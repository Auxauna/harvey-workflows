# Harvey GTM BA Operator Brief (90 Days)

## 1) Business Context
- Harvey serves legal and professional services teams with platform products including Assistant, Vault, Knowledge, Workflow Agents, and ecosystem integrations.
- Core GTM challenge this brief addresses: reduce execution friction while preserving trust, governance, and measurable ROI.
- Role framing: hybrid operator + builder (consultative business analysis first, automation second).

## 2) Current-State GTM System Map
- Operational systems: Salesforce, Marketo, Zendesk, Gong.
- Data + governance lane: Fivetran sync into Snowflake for cross-system KPI reporting and audits.
- Artifact lane: Notion (documentation and decision logs) + Linear (intake and execution tracking).
- Workflow lane: Vercel Workflow + AI SDK + AI Gateway for scoped automation.

## 3) 30/60/90 Cadence
- Days 1-30: diagnostic, ownership map, KPI baseline, prioritization rubric.
- Days 31-60: ship canaries for pre-call brief and routing with clear quality gates.
- Days 61-90: harden routing, run innovation pilot, and publish governance readouts.

## 4) Pilot Workflow Portfolio
- GTM-001: Pre-call brief (quick seller productivity win).
- GTM-002: Segment routing + human review queue (core system quality).
- GTM-003: Competitive mention assist (innovation pilot, explicitly scoped).

## 5) ROI + Governance Model
- Weekly: intake throughput, open risk log, SLA tracking.
- Biweekly: pilot quality/adoption go-no-go reviews.
- Monthly: KPI trend and roadmap decisions with leadership.

## Assumptions
- The claim "Harvey bought Vercel" is treated as unverified and excluded from core narrative.
- Stack details not publicly documented by Harvey are implementation assumptions to validate in Month 1.
